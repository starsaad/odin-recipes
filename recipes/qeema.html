Qeema Recipe

# Description

Qeema (also spelled keema) is a classic South Asian dish made from minced meat, typically beef, mutton, or chicken, cooked with a flavorful blend of spices. Itâ€™s a hearty and versatile dish,
often prepared with onions, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, and ginger, then spiced with ingredients like cumin, coriander, garam masala, and turmeric. Qeema can be dry or slightly saucy,
and it may include peas or potatoes for added texture and flavor. Served with roti, paratha, or rice, qeema is a comforting, everyday meal that's rich in taste and easy to adapt to personal preferences.

# Ingredients required:

  • 500g minced beef or mutton
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt, turmeric, chili powder
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)
