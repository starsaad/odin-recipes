Qeema Recipe

# Description

Qeema (also spelled keema) is a classic South Asian dish made from minced meat, typically beef, mutton, or chicken, cooked with a flavorful blend of spices. It's a hearty and versatile dish,
often prepared with onions, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, and ginger, then spiced with ingredients like cumin, coriander, garam masala, and turmeric. Qeema can be dry or slightly saucy,
and it may include peas or potatoes for added texture and flavor. Served with roti, paratha, or rice, qeema is a comforting, everyday meal that's rich in taste and easy to adapt to personal preferences.

# Ingredients required:

  • 500g minced beef or mutton
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt, turmeric, chili powder
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

How to make Qeema:

  1. Heat oil and saut√© chopped onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste; cook briefly.
  3. Stir in minced meat and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices (salt, turmeric, chili); mix well.
  5. Cook until tomatoes soften and oil separates.
  6. Simmer with a little water until meat is tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
